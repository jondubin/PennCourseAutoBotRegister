Open Course Notifications

Welcome to PennCourseAutoRegisterBot. To begin, use the form to add your email address, phone number, and courses you're interested in signing up for. When the courses open up, PennCourseAutoRegisterBot will attempt to sign you up for the course. We will notify you with a confirmation, or if there were any registration problems.

You can also use PennCourseAutoRegisterBot to look for open courses, or to get notified if courses open up, and not be AutoRegistered for them.
